(M)Ruby library that provides dynamic bindings to libraries that are GObject
See: mruby-girffi

Several MRBGEMS exist to extend the ruby-ness of said bindings.
They are:
  • mruby-gtk2 (doc)
  • mruby-glib2 (doc)
  • mruby-gobject (doc)
  • mruby-webkit-1 (doc)
  • mruby-gtk3 (doc)
  • mruby-webkit-3 (doc)
